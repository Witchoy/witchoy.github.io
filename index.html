About Me

I am a French student in software development, specialized in front-end and back-end development for the design and implementation of software solutions according to business needs.
My studies include the main programming languages, web development, system and network administration, database management, and project management. In my current specialization, "Réalisation d'applications : conception, développement, validation", I am acquiring skills in the development and maintenance of complex applications, performing automated testing, and ensuring continuous integration. These skills apply to various fields: web, mobile, finance, healthcare, and game development.
But beyond that, actually, what I learn is to create solutions for others to solve problems and improve their work lives. At the same time, I am deepening one of my biggest passions, video game development.

My Projects

    Torrington is a serious game developed by a team of three as part of a university project. The game aims to teach players about the process of selecting academic programs at a university. Built using Godot and C#, it integrates concepts from our management, law, and development courses, applying everything we learned throughout our years at uni.
